Timed PC Shutdown & Restart

For Linux: 

When Button is pressed enter a number in minutes.
Press Enter key
A shutdown will be scheduled for the time, after the amount
of minutes you entered.

Example: If the time is currently 12:00 and you entered 30,
when prompted, a shutdown will be scheduled for 12:30.

A notice will be displayed stating scheduled details

For Windows:

Same as linux, only user must enter seconds instead of minutes.

Example: If the time is currently 12:00 and you enter 60, a
shutdown will be scheduled for 12:01.


Cancelling Shutdown or Restart


For Linux:

Press Cancel Command button, all scheduled events will cancel.

For Windows:

Same procedure as Linux.
